Exploring UK Immigration Opportunities

This guide explores the various routes and visa options for migrating to the United Kingdom, highlighting key details for skilled workers, international students, and families.

UK Skilled Worker Visa Benefits

  • Visa holders can bring dependents.
  • Spouse is allowed to work.
  • No cap on number of migrants.
  • Minimum salary requirement reduced to £25,600.
  • Fast Track Visas for healthcare professionals.

UK Immigration Plan

New routes introduced, including the Global Business Mobility and Scale-up routes, aiming to attract talent and innovation.

Most Popular Ways to Migrate to the UK

  1. Through Work Visa - Skilled Migration.
  2. Through Student Route.
  3. Through Family Visa.
  4. Through UK Business Visa.
  5. Through UK Investor Visa.

UK Immigration Points-Based Assessment System

Candidates need to score a minimum of 70 points, with points awarded for job offers, salary, and English proficiency.

Visa Fee Details

  • Application Fees for stay up to 3 years: £610
  • Health surcharge: £624 per year

UK Permanent Resident (PR) Visa

Allows individuals to live and work in the UK indefinitely, with specific requirements for eligibility.
